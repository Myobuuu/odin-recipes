Pho Recipe

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat. Phở is a popular food in Vietnam where it is served in households, street-stalls, and restaurants country-wide. Residents of the city of Nam Định were the first to create Vietnamese traditional phở.

Ingredients

  • Chicken/Beef Broth
  • Chicken/Pork/Shrimp
  • 8 oz. package of dried rice vermicelli noodles
  • Green onions
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil leaves, mint leaves)
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Make a broth
  2. Prepare the noodles
  3. Prepare the garnishes
  4. Add meat to broth
  5. Assemble noodles to each bowl and ladle hot brother over top
  6. Add garnishes
